# Resume Repository Workflow

## Repository Map

- `resume/master-resume.md`: canonical long-form source
- `resume/resume-1page.md`: condensed general resume
- `resume/resume-2page.md`: broader condensed resume
- `resume/resume-manager.md`: management-leaning variant
- `resume/resume-staff.md`: staff/principal-leaning variant
- `accomplishments/`: fresh wins and recent evidence
- `portfolio/`: deeper project and achievement material
- `linkedin/`: profile wording that should stay aligned with resume claims
- `cover-letters/`: supporting narrative for role targeting

## Default Editing Order

1. Update `resume/master-resume.md`
2. Refresh the most relevant derived resume in `resume/`
3. Sync adjacent materials only when the user asks or when drift is obvious

## Tailoring Checklist

- Match summary wording to the target role
- Surface the most relevant technical domains first
- Prefer evidence of scale, ownership, and business impact
- Keep chronology intact
- Remove repeated bullets across sections
- Retain only truthful keywords supported by repository content

## Quality Checks

- Consistent tense within each role
- Consistent punctuation and bullet style
- No duplicated accomplishments in highlights and experience unless the repetition is intentional
- No unsupported metrics or claims
- Variant remains obviously derived from the master resume
